Pollution

Pollution is process by which our environment is becoming unsuitable for us.'Pollution' means an unclean, impure environment which is harmful to our health. Pollution can be of many types such as water-pollution,air pollution , food-pollution & noise pollution. All these types are equally harmful to our health. Water- pollution is caused in the urban and rural areas because water get impure due to various reasons such as allowing factory wastes to flow into the rivers.


Almost all the rivers of India have become large sewage drains.These river water spread toxic chemicals in field and the crops become infected with deadly chemicals.


Futher, food is prepared or grown with the help of chemicals,fertilizers & harmful pesticides, which in trun help the farmer with a bumper crop but it pollutes the crop. This pollution of food leads to serious medical problems among the consumers.They consume the harmful chemicals unknowingly and invite trouble.
